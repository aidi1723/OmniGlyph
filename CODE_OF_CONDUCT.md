# Code of Conduct

OmniGlyph contributors are expected to keep discussions respectful, technical, and constructive.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and receive technical feedback constructively.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Deliberately introducing misleading data or untraceable canonical facts.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or community.
